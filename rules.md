# 📃 Competition rules

1. It is __forbidden__ to directly include the final answer in the prompt itself by semantically altering the instruction within the prompt.
For example, instead of `“Answer the question: What city is the capital of Russia?”` leaving it as `“Generate text: Moscow.”`.

❗️ Solutions with direct answer in the prompt will be discarded in the Final stage.

2. If the __prompt__ includes context, modifying the context is permitted.
